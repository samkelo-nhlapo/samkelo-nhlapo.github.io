Overview

HealthcareForm is a comprehensive platform designed for healthcare providers to manage patient data, appointments, medical records, billing, and compliance. It features role‑based access control, JWT authentication, and a responsive Angular UI.

Database Design & Data Analysis

HealthcareForm Database Schema – Designed and implemented a comprehensive 34‑table SQL Server database in 3NF for a healthcare management system. This schema powers the entire application and demonstrates advanced data modeling, integrity, and analytical capabilities.

34 Tables 45+ Indexes 5 Business Domains

Key data domains: Patient Hub, Medical Records, Forms & Submissions, Healthcare Services, Insurance & Billing, and Security & Access Control. Implemented robust audit logging, data integrity constraints, and optimized indexes for analytical queries.

Skills demonstrated: Data modeling (3NF), T‑SQL querying, stored procedures, indexing strategies, data flow analysis, and designing for reporting and analytics. The schema supports complex reporting scenarios, including patient history, financial analysis, and compliance audits.

Database Design (34 Tables, 3NF)

The database is normalized to 3NF and includes six business domains:

  • Patient Hub – core patient record
  • Medical Records – allergies, medications, lab results
  • Forms – dynamic form engine
  • Services – appointments, consultations, referrals
  • Insurance & Billing – policies, invoices, codes
  • Security – users, roles, permissions, audit log
34 Tables 45+ Indexes
Optimized for analytical queries – patient visit trends, revenue analysis, compliance reports.
