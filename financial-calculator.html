💰 Financial Calculator

A Flutter mobile app to track income, expenses, and savings – built with clean architecture and local storage.

Overview

Ever wonder where your money goes each month? This app helps you get a clear picture of your finances. Track your income, expenses, and savings to see exactly what you have left to spend or save. It's not just a prototype – it's production‑ready with theme management, localization, and offline persistence.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and remove monthly income, expenses, and savings.
  • Real‑time calculation of disposable income.
  • Clean, card‑based UI with light/dark mode support (persisted).
  • Local storage using sqflite / shared_preferences – all data stays on device.
  • Shareable text summary of your finances.
  • Undo/redo history – deleted items go to a history screen for recovery.
  • Internationalization (i18n) ready – all text externalized.
  • Integrated logging for debugging and maintenance.

Architecture

The app follows a clean architecture with separation of concerns:

  • Data layer: Local database repositories.
  • Domain layer: Use cases and business logic.
  • Presentation layer: BLoC / Provider for state management, with reactive UI.

Technology Stack

Flutter Dart sqflite shared_preferences BLoC i18n
